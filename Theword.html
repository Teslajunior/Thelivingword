GOOD MORNING DEAR PEOPLE OF GOD

VERSE OF THE DAY
(John 15:19)
" If you were of the world the world will love its own; but because you are not of the world, but I choose you out of the world, therefore the world hates you. "

Yesterday "Seek first his kingdom and his righteousness" and today "you are not of the world". Ask yourself if I'm not of the world where then do I belong? Ours is his kingdom, we only need to seek it and it shall be ours, how difficult is that? Let us meditate on this words as we journey the day.

Have a blessed day.
